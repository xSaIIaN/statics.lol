Statics -  

Statics is a feature-rich Valorant companion app for Android, offering a seamless experience to track and share Valorant stats conveniently on mobile devices. With over 10,000 downloads from the Google Play Store, Statics stands out as a reliable tool for Valorant enthusiasts.

App Features

Log in with your Riot Games account to access your profile and services. This login ensures secure and convenient access to your games, progress, and community features provided by Riot Games.

Connect with gamers worldwide in a real-time environment using our build-in chat system. Share strategies, seek advice, and socialize within our integrated chat platform.

Access up to 100 past matches stored securely. Quickly retrieve and review your gameplay history to track your progress. Check your performance across various game modes.

Monitor your performance across various game modes with detailed metrics. Identify strengths and areas for improvement with ease. Enhance your strategic planning and team coordination.

Visualize player positions and spike plants in real-time. Enhance your strategic planning and team coordination. Identify strengths and areas for improvement with ease.

Manage multiple Valorant accounts effortlessly. Switch between accounts seamlessly without logging in and out. Keep track of your progress across multiple Valorant accounts.
